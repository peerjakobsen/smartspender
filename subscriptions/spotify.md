# Spotify

## Service Info
- **Website**: https://www.spotify.com
- **Category**: Streaming
- **Typical price**: 79-179 kr/month
- **Billing**: Monthly

## Cancellation Path
1. Navigate to: https://www.spotify.com/dk/account/subscription/
2. **[USER ACTION]**: Log in to the service
3. Navigate to: "Your plan" section
4. Click "Change plan"
5. Scroll to the bottom and click "Cancel Premium"
6. Spotify will present multiple retention screens -- continue through them
7. **[USER ACTION]**: Confirm cancellation by clicking "Yes, cancel"

## Retention Tactics
- Offers to downgrade to a cheaper plan (e.g., Individual instead of Duo/Family)
- May offer a discounted rate for 1-3 months
- Presents a pause option (up to 3 months) instead of full cancellation
- Reminds you of playlists and listening history -- these are preserved even on the free tier
- Decline all and proceed

## Quirks
- **Binding period**: None
- **Notice period**: None -- Premium continues until end of current billing period, then reverts to free tier
- **Refund policy**: No refunds for partial months
- Plans: Individual (79 kr), Duo (129 kr), Family up to 6 accounts (179 kr), Student (49 kr)
- Cancellation does not delete the account -- you keep a free Spotify account with ads
- Playlists, saved music, and followers are preserved on the free tier
- If subscribed through Apple App Store, cancel via Apple subscription settings instead
- Cannot cancel via the mobile app -- must use the website
