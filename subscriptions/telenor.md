# Telenor

## Service Info
- **Website**: https://www.telenor.dk
- **Category**: Telecom
- **Typical price**: 129-349 kr/month
- **Billing**: Monthly

## Cancellation Path
1. Navigate to: https://www.telenor.dk/mit-telenor
2. **[USER ACTION]**: Log in with MitID
3. Navigate to: "Abonnementer" (Subscriptions)
4. Select the subscription you want to cancel
5. Click "Opsig abonnement" (Cancel subscription)
6. Telenor will display your notice period and the effective cancellation date -- review the dates
7. **[USER ACTION]**: Confirm cancellation
8. Save or screenshot the cancellation confirmation with the effective end date

### Alternative: Cancel by Phone
1. Call Telenor customer service: 72 12 00 00
2. **[USER ACTION]**: Verify identity with the agent
3. Request cancellation of your subscription
4. Decline any retention offers (see Retention Tactics below)
5. Ask for written confirmation via email or SMS

## Retention Tactics
- Telenor may offer a discounted plan at a lower price tier to keep you as a customer
- Common offers: reduced monthly price for 3-6 months, free data upgrade, waived fees
- They may ask why you are leaving and suggest a plan that "better fits your needs"
- Decline clearly: "Nej tak, jeg vil gerne opsige mit abonnement" (No thanks, I want to cancel my subscription)

## Quirks
- **Binding period**: Typically 6 months on new contracts -- check your contract start date in MitTelenor
- **Notice period**: 30 days from the end of the current billing period
- **Refund policy**: No refunds for partial months -- service runs until the notice period ends
- Number portability: Request your "porteringskode" (porting code) in MitTelenor before cancelling if you want to keep your number
- If you cancel before the binding period ends, you may be charged for the remaining months
- Plans: Telenor Fri (129 kr), Telenor Fri+ (179 kr), Telenor Fri++ (249 kr), Telenor Max (349 kr) -- prices vary with promotions
- Family/multi-SIM discounts may apply -- cancelling one line could affect pricing on other lines in the same group
