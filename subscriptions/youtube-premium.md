# YouTube Premium

## Service Info
- **Website**: https://www.youtube.com/premium
- **Category**: Streaming
- **Typical price**: 129 kr/month
- **Billing**: Both

## Cancellation Path
1. Navigate to: https://myaccount.google.com/subscriptions
2. **[USER ACTION]**: Log in to your Google account
3. Find "YouTube Premium" in the list of subscriptions
4. Click "Manage" next to YouTube Premium
5. Click "Cancel subscription" or "Deactivate"
6. Google will present retention options -- decline and continue
7. Select a cancellation reason if prompted
8. **[USER ACTION]**: Confirm cancellation

## Retention Tactics
- Offers to pause the subscription for 1-6 months instead of cancelling
- May offer a discounted rate for a limited period
- Highlights features you will lose (ad-free, background play, YouTube Music)
- Decline all and proceed to confirm

## Quirks
- **Binding period**: None for monthly; yearly plans run to end of term
- **Notice period**: None -- access continues until end of current billing period
- **Refund policy**: No refunds for partial months; yearly plans may be partially refundable depending on region
- Plans: Individual (129 kr), Family up to 5 accounts (239 kr), Student (79 kr)
- Cancelling YouTube Premium also cancels YouTube Music Premium (they are bundled)
- Managed through Google subscription settings, not YouTube directly
- If subscribed through Apple App Store, cancel via Apple Settings > Subscriptions
- Downloaded videos for offline use will become unavailable after cancellation
