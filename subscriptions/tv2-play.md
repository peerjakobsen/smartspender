# TV2 Play

## Service Info
- **Website**: https://play.tv2.dk
- **Category**: Streaming
- **Typical price**: 79-149 kr/month
- **Billing**: Monthly

## Cancellation Path
1. Navigate to: https://play.tv2.dk/profil
2. **[USER ACTION]**: Log in to the service (uses TV2 Play login or MitID for some features)
3. Navigate to: "Abonnement" (Subscription) section
4. Click "Opsig abonnement" (Cancel Subscription)
5. Complete the cancellation survey if prompted
6. **[USER ACTION]**: Confirm cancellation

## Retention Tactics
- May offer a discounted month or a free trial period for a higher-tier plan
- Shows a summary of Danish content you will lose access to
- Decline all offers and confirm

## Quirks
- **Binding period**: None for standard monthly plans
- **Notice period**: Cancel before next billing date to avoid being charged for the following month
- **Refund policy**: No refunds for partial months
- Plans: TV2 Play Basis (79 kr), TV2 Play Favorit (119 kr), TV2 Play Premium (149 kr)
- Some content is free with ads on tv2.dk without a subscription
- If subscribed through a TV provider (YouSee, Stofa, etc.), cancellation must go through that provider
- Danish-only service -- primarily Danish content including news, drama, and sport
