# Zetland

## Service Info
- **Website**: https://www.zetland.dk
- **Category**: News
- **Typical price**: 129 kr/month
- **Billing**: Monthly

## Cancellation Path
1. Navigate to: https://www.zetland.dk/konto
2. **[USER ACTION]**: Log in with your Zetland credentials
3. Navigate to: "Abonnement" (Subscription)
4. Click "Opsig abonnement" (Cancel Subscription)
5. Select a reason for cancellation from the list
6. **[USER ACTION]**: Confirm cancellation
7. Save the confirmation email as proof of cancellation

## Retention Tactics
- May show a summary of how many articles you have read and how much journalism you have supported
- May offer a reduced price (e.g. 79 kr/month) for a limited period to stay
- May suggest switching to a cheaper "supporter" tier instead of cancelling entirely
- Zetland's cancellation flow is generally straightforward and not overly aggressive
- Decline any offers if the goal is to cancel

## Quirks
- **Binding period**: None. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time.
- **Notice period**: None -- access continues until end of current billing period.
- **Refund policy**: No refunds for partial months.
- **Pricing model**: Zetland uses a single-tier model at approximately 129 kr/month. They occasionally run "pay what you want" campaigns or member-referred discounts.
- **Member-driven journalism**: Zetland is a member-funded publication. They may frame cancellation around supporting independent journalism, but this does not affect the cancellation process.
- **Easy cancellation**: Zetland is known for a clean, no-friction cancellation process. No phone calls required, no multi-step retention funnels.
- **Reactivation**: Account can be reactivated at any time. Reading history and preferences are preserved.
