# Viaplay

## Service Info
- **Website**: https://viaplay.dk
- **Category**: Streaming
- **Typical price**: 99-199 kr/month
- **Billing**: Monthly

## Cancellation Path
1. Navigate to: https://viaplay.dk/account
2. **[USER ACTION]**: Log in to the service
3. Navigate to: "Mit abonnement" (My Subscription)
4. Click "Opsig abonnement" (Cancel Subscription)
5. Viaplay will present retention offers and ask for a reason -- decline and continue
6. **[USER ACTION]**: Confirm cancellation

## Retention Tactics
- Offers a discounted price for 1-3 months to retain you
- May suggest downgrading to a cheaper package
- Highlights sports events or exclusive content you will lose access to
- Be firm and decline all offers

## Quirks
- **Binding period**: Some sport packages have binding periods of up to 6 months -- check your specific plan
- **Notice period**: Cancellation must be submitted before the next billing date; otherwise you are charged for the next period
- **Refund policy**: No refunds for partial months; binding period plans cannot be cancelled early without paying remaining months
- Plans: Basis (99 kr), Standard (149 kr), Premium/Sport (199 kr)
- Viaplay has undergone restructuring -- package names and prices may change
- If subscribed through a telecom bundle (e.g., YouSee, Telia), cancel through the telecom provider instead
