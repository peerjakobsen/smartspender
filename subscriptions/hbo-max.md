# HBO Max

## Service Info
- **Website**: https://www.max.com
- **Category**: Streaming
- **Typical price**: 99-149 kr/month
- **Billing**: Both

## Cancellation Path
1. Navigate to: https://www.max.com
2. **[USER ACTION]**: Log in to the service
3. Click the profile icon in the top-right corner
4. Select "Account" from the dropdown
5. Navigate to: Subscription section
6. Click "Manage Subscription" or "Cancel Subscription"
7. Follow the prompts -- decline any retention offers
8. **[USER ACTION]**: Confirm cancellation

## Retention Tactics
- May offer a discounted month or temporary pause instead of cancellation
- Shows a list of upcoming content you will miss
- Decline all offers and proceed to confirm

## Quirks
- **Binding period**: None for monthly plans; yearly plans run to term
- **Notice period**: None for monthly
- **Refund policy**: No refunds for partial months; yearly plans are non-refundable once started
- Rebranded from HBO Max to Max in 2023 -- URLs and branding may reference either name
- Plans: Basic with ads (99 kr), Standard (149 kr)
- If subscribed through Apple/Google, cancellation must happen through that platform's subscription settings
