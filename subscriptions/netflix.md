# Netflix

## Service Info
- **Website**: https://www.netflix.com
- **Category**: Streaming
- **Typical price**: 79-179 kr/month
- **Billing**: Monthly

## Cancellation Path
1. Navigate to: https://www.netflix.com/account
2. **[USER ACTION]**: Log in to the service
3. Navigate to: Membership & Billing section
4. Click "Cancel Membership"
5. Netflix will show a page explaining what you will lose -- ignore the prompts
6. Click "Finish Cancellation"
7. **[USER ACTION]**: Confirm cancellation

## Retention Tactics
- Netflix may offer to downgrade to a cheaper plan instead of cancelling
- A summary screen shows your "last day of access" to create urgency
- Decline all offers and proceed with "Finish Cancellation"

## Quirks
- **Binding period**: None
- **Notice period**: None -- access continues until end of current billing period
- **Refund policy**: No refunds for partial months
- Plans: Standard with ads (79 kr), Standard (149 kr), Premium (179 kr)
- Account can be reactivated at any time with full profile history preserved
