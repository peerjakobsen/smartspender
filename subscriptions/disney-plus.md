# Disney+

## Service Info
- **Website**: https://www.disneyplus.com
- **Category**: Streaming
- **Typical price**: 89-149 kr/month
- **Billing**: Both

## Cancellation Path
1. Navigate to: https://www.disneyplus.com/account
2. **[USER ACTION]**: Log in to the service
3. Navigate to: Subscription section
4. Click "Manage" next to your subscription
5. Click "Cancel Subscription"
6. Disney+ will present a survey and retention offers -- skip through them
7. **[USER ACTION]**: Confirm cancellation by clicking "Complete Cancellation"

## Retention Tactics
- Presents a cancellation survey asking why you are leaving
- May offer a discounted rate or temporary pause
- Shows upcoming releases to create fear of missing out
- Decline all offers and complete the cancellation flow

## Quirks
- **Binding period**: None for monthly; yearly plans run to end of term
- **Notice period**: None
- **Refund policy**: No refunds for partial months; yearly plans are non-refundable
- Plans: Standard with ads (89 kr), Standard (129 kr), Premium (149 kr)
- If subscribed through Apple App Store or Google Play, cancel through that platform instead
- Profiles and watchlists are preserved for a period after cancellation in case you resubscribe
