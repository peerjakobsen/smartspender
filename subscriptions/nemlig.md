# Nemlig.com (Nemlig Plus)

## Service Info
- **Website**: https://www.nemlig.com
- **Category**: Delivery
- **Typical price**: 79 kr/month
- **Billing**: Monthly

## Cancellation Path
1. Navigate to: https://www.nemlig.com/mine-sider
2. **[USER ACTION]**: Log in with your Nemlig.com account
3. Navigate to: "Mine sider" (My Pages) > "Nemlig Plus"
4. Click "Administrer abonnement" (Manage subscription)
5. Click "Opsig Nemlig Plus" (Cancel Nemlig Plus)
6. Select a reason for cancellation from the options presented
7. **[USER ACTION]**: Confirm cancellation by clicking "Bekraeft opsigelse"
8. Save the confirmation email as proof of cancellation

## Retention Tactics
- May display a summary of delivery savings you have accumulated as a Nemlig Plus member
- May offer a discounted rate or a free month to stay
- May suggest trying a different delivery window or reducing order frequency instead of cancelling
- Decline all offers firmly if the goal is to cancel

## Quirks
- **Binding period**: None
- **Notice period**: None -- access continues until end of current billing period
- **Refund policy**: No refunds for partial months
- **Delivery benefit**: Nemlig Plus provides free delivery on all orders (standard delivery fee without Plus is typically 39-59 kr per order depending on time slot)
- **Minimum order**: Nemlig.com has a minimum order amount (typically around 250 kr) regardless of Plus membership
- **Delivery area**: Nemlig.com delivers primarily in Sjaelland, parts of Fyn, and eastern Jylland. Check their website for current coverage.
- Account and order history are preserved after cancellation. You can resubscribe at any time.
